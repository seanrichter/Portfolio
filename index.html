About Me

Hello, my name is Sean! I enjoy moonlight strolls on the beach, red roses by the armful, and tap-dancing the night away.

Just kidding! Im not a fan of any of those things except the occasional night of dancing under the right circumstances. More seriously, my friends would describe me as easy going, a good listener, wordsmith at scrabble, and someone who can whip up a delicious breakfast.

I have many hobbies that include music, golfing, and fishing. My most recent hobby has become web development. I had a friend do a similar bootcamp a few years ago. Before my friend tried this, i actually had no idea what coding/web development was!! So here i am, nearly 2/3 into my own bootcamp experience. I am nowhere near where i would like to be when it comes to web development. However, i am a dedicated individual that has the initiative and passion to learn something new!
