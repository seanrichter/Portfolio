About Me

Hello, my name is Sean! I enjoy moonlight strolls on the beach, red roses by the armful, and tap-dancing the night away.

Just kidding! Im not a fan of any of those things except the occasional night of dancing under the right circumstances. More seriously, my friends would describe me as easy going, a good listener, wordsmith at scrabble, and someone who can whip up a delicious breakfast.

I have many hobbies that include music, golfing, and fishing. My most recent hobby has become web development. I had a friend do a similar bootcamp a few years ago. Before my friend did this, i actually had never heard of coding/web development!! i knew somebody out there was magically creating web content but no idea how they were doing it. So here i am today presenting to you some of my finished and unfinished work. I am nowhere near where i would like to be when it comes to fullstack development. However, i am a dedicated individual that has the initiative and passion to learn something new!

Please feel free to take a look at my Portfolio and Skills. Also, drop me a message in the Contacts Page if you want to critique. Thank you for visiting!!
